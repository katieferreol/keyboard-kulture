Keycaps.

The plastic covers that you place on top of the switch.

OEM

Sculpted, different heights for each row. Molds with your fingers during typing and gaming.

DSA

Has same height across all rows. Uniform profile may have an adjustment period required.

Cherry

Most common; 1 millimeter shorter than others. Allows easy movement from one key to another.

SA

Taller and angled from the top. Has spherical shape that Provides softer aesthetic and typing.
